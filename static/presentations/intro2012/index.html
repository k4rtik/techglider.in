T-Shaped
Model
Be a generalist:
(highly skilled at a broad
set of valuable things)
- the top of the T

T's top helps in team work
T's leg involves individual contribution
Courtesy: Valve, Handbook for New Employees
Be an expert:
(among the best in your field within a narrow discipline)
- the vertical leg of the T
