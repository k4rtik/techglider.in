WELCOME TO DAV PUBLIC SCHOOL


 

From the Desk of the Principal

Dear Children,

Within the last couple of years, the school has grown by leaps and bounds-both in terms of infrastructure and Academic/Co-curricular Activities. In the last academic Session our School was given the ISO 9001:2000 Certificate by D.N.V. Netherlands for its standard at par with International Schools and in this Academic Session(2004-05) the Certificate was renewed by the auditors during surveillance audit. This is no small achievement. Aquiring a much coveted label (ISO 9001:2000) is a big thing, maintaining it, is a still greater achievement.

The School buildings have taken a very definite shape, with much more in the offing viz a separate Primary Wing, a Swimming Pool and a Multipurpose Hall. The Principal's Office and the whole of the Administrative Staff alongwith the Library and the Computer Lab have shifted to the new Administrative Block which is now facing the main road. All our achievements have been possible with the help of Local Managing Committee and co-operation of a dedicated team of staff and Parents. I take this opportunity to thank each of you for assisting me in making this eandeavour a success.

Above all, I am indebted to my seniors in the DAV College Managing Committee, who have encouraged and guided me in every step of the way to make this institution the grand success that it has become today.

O. P. Mehta
Principal
