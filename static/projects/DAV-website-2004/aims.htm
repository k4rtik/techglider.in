AIMS AND OBJECTIVES OF THE SCHOOL

  1. To help the children in all round development of personality viz physical, mental, social and spiritual.

  2. To develop a sense of enlightened citizenship.

  3. To develop in them a sense of value, team spirit, nationalism, scientific and democratic attitude.

  4. To help the students in developing social skills and attitudes.

  5. To prepare them for their future career.

  6. To cultivate in them, love and appreciation for their culture and traditions.
